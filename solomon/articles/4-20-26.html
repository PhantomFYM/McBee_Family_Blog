~Musical Dares~

This is an idea I had for when I can code well enough: You must play musical chairs with a card that you must follow. You play it with either computers or real players. There are two types of cards, the first of which is Nail Cards: These cards last the whole game. These cards are more mild, It could be anything from being further from all chairs, to making the game 2x speed. Then, there are Hammer Cards: These are the bad cards. These only last one turn, but they do some real damage. This could be anything from having only 1 of the chairs being possible to sit on, to being blind that round.

Musical dares is different from musical chairs as follows. The game starts with 100 people, and each round ends when 10 people die, each round the chairs get mixed around as well as all of the players. When the music is on everyone runs from enemies while dodging obstacles. Though, the music randomly stops, so dodging obstacles could be tricky.
