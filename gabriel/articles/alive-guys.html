I walked around the corner then OOF 😭 I banged into a wall. I ran down the street crying in tears. The tears became… a-alive!? I was so confused and curious at the same time. I tried to see what was causing this, and nothing. I was sure it would just stop but NO. How? It in fact made my sweat and drool come alive. This was confusing.

NOW WHEN I EAT MY FOOD TURNS ALIVE!

I can’t eat now! You wanna know what it said to me? “STOP EATING!” I’m mad now, I will stop this!

I try to retrace my steps. I see a clue, a big one. I found a bug, I didn’t know which bug it was so I asked dad if I could use his phone. He said “sure”, so I searched it up and it was a “Life Bug”. It said that these bugs force you to make life juice. It also said that if you have been bit by one, to stop producing life juice you have to squish the bug. This wasn’t the same bug that bit me though. So I ran ran ran ran ran and ran so long that I found a new one.

It was the one! :) I was gonna kill it until it said “Please… don’t kill me…” so I didn’t. I asked why it didn’t just leave me alone and it said “That’s not how it works” and so we became friends.

THE END :)
