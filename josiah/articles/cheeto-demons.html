Cheeto Puff Demons! - 5/6/2026

Josiah's Page

One day, my mom got cheetos. I went to get some cheetos, but they were actually cheeto puffs. I was expecting to eat some cheetos, so when I saw this, I cried. I also thought that these cheetos were cheeto demons. My mom said she would get regular cheetos next time. A bit later, mom got regular cheetos. I ate these cheetos, and finally I had been happy again, after the time I had got cheeto puffs and was not. I wanted to tell my mom thank you. Then, I had finally eaten my cheetos, and I had said thanks for them! The end.
